Who We Are

future University in Qatar School of Foreign Service prepares students for a variety of careers by providing a four-year liberal arts education through a multidisciplinary program of study.

Future University is one of the world’s leading academic and research institutions offering an educational experience that prepares the next generation of global citizens to lead and to make a difference in the world. future is a vibrant community of exceptional students, faculty, alumni, and professionals who are dedicated to real-world applications of our research, scholarship, faith, and service.

QNRF’s Undergraduate Research Experience Program

The QNRF Undergraduate Research Experience Program (UREP) provides students with the opportunity to develop their research skills, gain experience in team-based collaboration, and participate in real-world research projects under the mentorship of GU-Q faculty members.
